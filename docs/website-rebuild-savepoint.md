## Core Strategy

The website is not just an information archive. It should be designed for real human decision-making.

Each strong page should reduce cognitive load, create visual trust, answer the buyer's next question, reduce perceived risk, and move the visitor toward a low-pressure conversation.

## Page Strategy

- Home: identity, first trust, product direction, project proof, next-step routing.
- Supply Scope: explain factory-side supply package, product family choice, components, boundaries, and low-friction inquiry path.
- Lumina Slate: premium glass tile product line.
- Storm Guard: modern metal sheet product line.
- Projects: social proof and visual trust.
- Project Detail: project credibility and case-study depth.
- Contact: light first conversation; no drawing required for first message.

## Design Principle

Do not design for AI information completeness first. Design for human scanning behavior, psychological risk reduction, and staged persuasion.
