Project Title (网页主标题):

Urban Eco-Park Energy Station: A Comprehensive 360° BIPV Showcase

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Beyond the Roof: The municipality required a zero-emission utility substation for a new urban wetland park. The goal was twofold: the building needed to be 100% self-sufficient, and it had to serve as a public educational landmark for green energy. Traditional solar panels were visually unacceptable for a park setting. Furthermore, the limited roof footprint meant that power generation had to be expanded beyond just the roof to meet the energy demands.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

The "All-in-One" Building Envelope: We delivered a total BIPV solution that transformed the entire structure into a 360-degree power generator.

The Roof: The pitched section utilizes our aesthetic solar roof tiles, offering a sleek, traditional architectural look.

The Canopy: The flat extension features transparent solar glass, allowing natural light to penetrate while generating power.

The Facade: The vertical walls are clad in our specialized solar curtain wall modules—some featuring a sleek dark finish, while others utilize custom-printed glass to blend seamlessly with the park's scenery.

This pilot project perfectly demonstrates our comprehensive engineering and supply chain capabilities, proving we can solarize every inch of a building's exterior.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数 (综合类展示):

System Type: Comprehensive BIPV Demonstration Station

Integrated Modules: Aesthetic Solar Tiles (Roof), Transparent Solar Glass (Canopy), Solar Curtain Wall (Facade)

Key Features: 360-degree building envelope integration, custom-printed solar glass, off-grid public utility powering.

Application: Smart City Infrastructure, Eco-Parks, Commercial Pavilions.
