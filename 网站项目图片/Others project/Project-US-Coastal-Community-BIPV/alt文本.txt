Project Title (网页主标题):

US Coastal Community: Neighborhood-Scale BIPV Deployment

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

The Developer & HOA Dilemma: Housing developers in premium coastal communities face a significant challenge. They want to offer energy-independent, green homes to attract high-end buyers, but standard rack-mounted solar panels are often rejected by strict Homeowner Association (HOA) aesthetic guidelines because they look industrial and cluttered. The challenge was to deliver a uniform, high-capacity solar solution across multiple newly built properties without compromising the clean, modern architectural harmony of the neighborhood.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Developer-Ready Standard Integration: We partnered with the project developers to deploy our Lumina Slate series as the standard roofing material for this waterfront community. As shown in the aerial footage, the deep black solar tiles provide a striking, premium contrast to the bright coastal facades and surrounding palm trees.
HOA Compliant & Value-Boosting: By acting as the primary roof itself, the system effortlessly bypasses strict HOA aesthetic restrictions. Each home now operates as an independent clean power plant (averaging 10-15kW per household) while collectively elevating the entire neighborhood's curb appeal and property values.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数 (基于小区统装预估):

System Type: Community-Scale Residential Development

Capacity: 10-15 kWp per household

Key Features: HOA-compliant aesthetic, neighborhood-uniformity, developer-ready scalability.

Application Environment: High-end coastal/waterfront communities.
