Project Title (网页主标题):

Heritage Aesthetic Meets Green Energy: Custom Red BIPV Solutions

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Strict Aesthetic and Heritage Restrictions: Many high-end communities, resorts, and historical districts have strict architectural guidelines requiring traditional terracotta or red clay roofs. In these zones, standard blue or black solar panels are strictly prohibited as they destroy the visual harmony of the architecture. The client needed a renewable energy solution that could entirely disguise itself as a traditional red roof without compromising power output.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Perfect Color Matching (Custom Colored BIPV): We manufactured and deployed a custom-colored red BIPV system specifically engineered for this project. To achieve a 100% uniform aesthetic, we supplied high-efficiency red active solar tiles alongside perfectly color-matched inactive (dummy) tiles for complex edges, cuts, and non-solar areas.
Architectural Harmony: As shown in the aerial photos, the result is a stunning, vibrant red roof that effortlessly mimics traditional terracotta. This 15kW pilot project generates around 16,500 kWh annually, proving that high-performance solar can seamlessly adapt to the most demanding aesthetic standards.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数 (基于多坡面大体量预估):

System Type: Custom Colored BIPV (Commercial / Heritage Residential)

Solar Tiles: Custom Terracotta Red Series

Estimated Capacity: 15 kWp

Key Features: Perfect color matching, active & inactive (dummy) tile integration, heritage building compliance.

Annual Estimated Output: 16,500 kWh
