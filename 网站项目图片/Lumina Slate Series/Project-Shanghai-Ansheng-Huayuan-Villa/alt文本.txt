Project Title (网页主标题):

Shanghai Ansheng Huayuan Villa: Modernizing Urban Residences with BIPV

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Urban Roof Retrofitting: This project involved upgrading an existing luxury villa in a mature Shanghai neighborhood. The homeowner wanted to transition to green energy to cover their high electricity usage. However, traditional rack-mounted panels would have clashed terribly with the classic white-walled architecture and the established aesthetic of the community.
Space Constraints: With a relatively compact roof area of 140 sq.m featuring dormer windows and multi-level sections, maximizing the power capacity to 6kW required a highly space-efficient solution.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Aesthetic Property Upgrade: We completely retrofitted the roof using the Lumina Slate (Pro) series. The deep, sleek black of the solar slates creates a striking, modern contrast against the villa's white facade. As seen in the project photos, this BIPV upgrade instantly elevates the property's curb appeal, making it stand out beautifully against the aging traditional roofs of neighboring houses.
Compact Power: By utilizing the entire roof surface edge-to-edge, including the areas around the dormer windows, we successfully achieved the 6kW capacity goal. The system now reliably generates 7,200 kWh annually, completely invisible to the naked eye.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数:

System Type: Grid-Tied Residential (Retrofit)

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro)

Installed Capacity: 6 kWp

Active Roof Area: 140 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 7,200 kWh

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 6.98 tons, equivalent to planting 388 trees annually.
