Project Title (网页主标题):

Shanghai Hongqiao Royal Villa: High-Density Solar Power for Premium Estates

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

High Energy Demand vs. Limited Space: Luxury estates often have massive electricity consumption due to central climate control and advanced smart home systems. This client needed a massive 22kW power capacity. However, the usable roof area was only 168 sq.m. Standard solar panels simply could not generate enough power within such a limited footprint.
Strict Community Aesthetic Rules: Located in the exclusive Hongqiao Royal Villa community, all houses share a unified classical architectural style. Slapping industrial metal solar racks onto the roof would violate community aesthetic standards and ruin the estate's high-end curb appeal.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Maximum Efficiency, Zero Visual Clutter: We utilized the high-efficiency Lumina Slate (Pro+) series. By acting as the actual roof and covering the space edge-to-edge—seamlessly wrapping around dormers and chimneys—we successfully achieved the massive 22kW capacity requirement within the 168 sq.m footprint.
A Neighborhood Standout: Instead of detracting from the community's look, the sleek, dark slate finish actually modernizes the classic stone facade. It stands as a pinnacle of sustainable luxury, silently generating an impressive 23,000 kWh annually while looking better than the traditional roofs surrounding it.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数:

System Type: Grid-Tied Ultra-Luxury Residential

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro+)

Installed Capacity: 22 kWp (High-Density)

Active Roof Area: 168 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 23,000 kWh

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 21.3 tons, equivalent to planting over 1,180 trees annually.
