Project Title (网页主标题):

Hangzhou Xianghu Arcadia Villa: Seamless Solar Integration in Scenic Nature

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Zero Visual Pollution in Scenic Areas: This luxury villa is nestled in the picturesque Xianghu Arcadia region, surrounded by lush green mountains. The homeowner desired a 10kW solar system but strictly prohibited traditional blue panels that would clash with the elegant grey-brick architecture and destroy the harmony of the natural landscape.
Architectural Obstacles: The roof design included complex features such as arched dormer windows, cross-shaped decorative vents, and a massive glass skylight cluster, requiring absolute precision to ensure both waterproofing and aesthetic continuity.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Nature-Harmonious Aesthetics: By deploying the Lumina Slate (Pro) series, we transformed the 227 sq.m roof into a completely invisible power plant. The sleek, dark finish of the solar tiles perfectly complements the classic grey brickwork, blending so naturally that it looks like premium traditional slate.
Precision Flashing & Integration: Our engineering team custom-fitted the BIPV modules around the prominent skylights and dormer windows. The flush design allows leaves and rainwater to slide off easily, maintaining a pristine look while generating a reliable 12,000 kWh of clean energy annually.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数:

System Type: Grid-Tied Residential

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro)

Installed Capacity: 10 kWp

Active Roof Area: 227 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 12,000 kWh

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 11.3 tons, equivalent to planting 630 trees annually.
