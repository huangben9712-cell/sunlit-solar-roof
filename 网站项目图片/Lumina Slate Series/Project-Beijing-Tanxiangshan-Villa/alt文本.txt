Project Title (网页主标题):

Beijing Tanxiangshan Villa: High-Capacity BIPV Roofing Solutions

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Maximized Capacity on a Traditional Profile: The client owned a sprawling luxury villa with a massive 266+ sq.m roof. The primary goal was to maximize the solar generating capacity (reaching 13kW) to cover high household energy demands, without resorting to ugly traditional solar racks that would ruin the neighborhood's aesthetic.
Flawless Wire Management: With such a large-scale residential installation, ensuring structural waterproofing and safe, hidden electrical routing was a major concern for the client and the local building authorities.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Staggered Layout & Seamless Integration: We utilized the Lumina Slate (Pro+) series, employing a classic staggered installation pattern. This not only enhanced the modern architectural beauty—creating a stunning contrast with neighboring traditional roofs—but also optimized water runoff.
Engineered Beneath the Surface: As our installation logs show, the system features a robust structural batten framework and meticulous underlying DC wire management, ensuring that the 15,600 kWh annual generation is both completely invisible and exceptionally safe.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数:

System Type: Grid-Tied Residential

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro+)

Installed Capacity: 13 kWp

Active Roof Area: 266.77 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 15,600 kWh

Financial Return (25 Yrs): Approx. $41,500 USD (Equiv. 297,000 RMB)

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 13.29 tons, equivalent to planting 740 trees annually.
