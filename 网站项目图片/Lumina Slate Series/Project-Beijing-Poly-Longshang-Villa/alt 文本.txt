Project Title (网页主标题):

Beijing Poly Longshang Luxury Villa: BIPV Integration on Complex Roof Geometry

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Complex Roof Geometry & Obstacles: The villa features a highly sophisticated multi-hip roof design, punctuated by chimneys and large skylights. Traditional rectangular solar panels cannot efficiently cover these irregular trapezoidal roof faces without leaving awkward gaps and wasting valuable space.
Aesthetic Preservation: Located in a high-end residential community, the client demanded a 13kW high-capacity solar system but absolutely refused the visually disruptive, bulky metal frames of traditional BAPV systems.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Edge-to-Edge Modular Fit: We deployed the Lumina Slate (Pro+) series. Its modular design and custom flashing allowed our installation team to navigate seamlessly around the chimneys and skylights. By covering the irregular facets edge-to-edge, we maximized the active roof area to 255 sq.m.
The Invisible Solar Roof: The finished roof maintains a sleek, modern, flush aesthetic that perfectly matches the villa's high-end architectural style, while seamlessly generating 15,600 kWh annually.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数:

System Type: Grid-Tied Residential

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro+)

Installed Capacity: 13 kWp

Active Roof Area: 255 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 15,600 kWh

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 12.7 tons, equivalent to planting 707 trees annually.
