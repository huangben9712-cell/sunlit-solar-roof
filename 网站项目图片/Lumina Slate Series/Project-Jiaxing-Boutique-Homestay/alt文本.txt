Project Title (网页主标题):

Jiaxing Boutique Homestay: Elevating Hospitality with Invisible Solar

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Aesthetics as a Core Business Asset: For boutique homestays and luxury guesthouses, the visual appeal and atmosphere are what attract guests. The property owner wanted to offset high electricity costs but knew that installing traditional, bulky solar panels (BAPV) would ruin the carefully curated architectural vibe and potentially alienate customers.
High Energy Demand: Operating a hospitality business requires significant power. The client needed a robust 8kW system packed into a moderate 180 sq.m roof footprint.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Eco-Tourism Branding with Zero Visual Pollution: We integrated the Lumina Slate (Pro) series to serve as the building's primary roof. The minimalist, dark slate finish seamlessly blends with the local architecture while quietly turning the entire roof into a power generator.
A Sustainable Destination: Generating 9,600 kWh of clean energy annually, the homestay now successfully markets itself as an "eco-friendly destination"—a major selling point for modern travelers—proving that sustainability and premium aesthetics can go hand in hand.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数:

System Type: Commercial / Hospitality

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro)

Installed Capacity: 8 kWp

Active Roof Area: 180 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 9,600 kWh

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 8.97 tons, equivalent to planting 499 trees annually.
