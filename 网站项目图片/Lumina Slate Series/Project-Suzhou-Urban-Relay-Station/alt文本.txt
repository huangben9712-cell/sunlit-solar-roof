Project Title (网页主标题):

Suzhou Urban Relay Station: Empowering Smart City Infrastructure

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Public Safety & Urban Aesthetics: This project is a newly constructed public relay station located in a busy urban district in Suzhou. Public utilities demand extreme durability and public safety. Traditional solar panels with exposed wiring present safety and vandalism risks, and their industrial look contradicts the city's modern urban planning guidelines.
Self-Sustaining Operations: The municipal government required the pavilion to generate its own clean energy to power lighting and public amenities, but the technology had to remain completely unobtrusive.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Tamper-Resistant Invisible Power: We specified the Lumina Slate (Pro) series for this smart city infrastructure. Acting as the primary roof covering, the system provides a sleek, flush surface with zero exposed wiring—eliminating public safety hazards.
A Modern Landmark: The deep black solar slates create a striking, futuristic contrast with the pavilion's white structural frame. As seen against the cluttered traditional roofs of the older residential buildings nearby, this 8kW BIPV installation not only powers the station (generating 8,500 kWh annually) but also elevates the entire streetscape, setting a new standard for modern civic architecture.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数 (合理预估):

System Type: Commercial / Public Infrastructure

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro)

Estimated Capacity: 8 kWp

Estimated Active Area: 85 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 8,500 kWh

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 8.4 tons, equivalent to planting 468 trees annually.
