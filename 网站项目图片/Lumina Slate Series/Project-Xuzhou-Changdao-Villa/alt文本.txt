Project Title (网页主标题):

Xuzhou Changdao Villa: BIPV vs. Traditional Solar Comparison

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Avoiding the "Industrial" Look: The homeowner wanted a high-capacity solar system for their premium villa. However, observing the neighboring properties equipped with traditional rack-mounted panels (BAPV), they noticed the severe visual disruption these systems caused. The bulky metal frames completely ruined the neighborhood's upscale architecture. The client's goal was clear: achieve energy independence without turning their home into an industrial site.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

A Clear Visual Superiority: We installed a 13kW Lumina Slate (Pro) system across the 140.6 sq.m multi-hip roof. As our aerial site photos clearly demonstrate, the contrast is undeniable. While neighboring houses suffer from elevated, cluttered racks, the Sunlit roof maintains a flat, elegant slate appearance.
Practical Integration: The system seamlessly wraps around the complex roof ridges and perfectly integrates with existing vent pipes, producing a reliable 14,000 kWh annually while actively protecting the home's aesthetic and market value.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数:

System Type: Grid-Tied Residential

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro)

Installed Capacity: 13 kWp

Active Roof Area: 140.6 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 14,000 kWh

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 7 tons, equivalent to planting 390 trees annually.
