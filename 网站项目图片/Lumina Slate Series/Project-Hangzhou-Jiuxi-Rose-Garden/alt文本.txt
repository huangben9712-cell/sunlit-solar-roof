Project Title (网页主标题):

Hangzhou Jiuxi Rose Garden: Powering Ultra-Luxury European Estates

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Preserving Classical Elegance: The Jiuxi Rose Garden is one of the most prestigious ultra-luxury residential areas. This specific estate features a grand European classical design, complete with stone facades, Roman columns, and intricate archways. The owner required a massive solar energy system to power the large estate but absolutely refused to install standard blue solar panels that would instantly ruin the chateau's timeless, aristocratic aesthetic.
High Energy Demand: A mansion of this scale has tremendous electricity requirements for climate control, lighting, and estate management, requiring a maximum-capacity rooftop solution.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

The Modern Slate for Classic Architecture: We specified the Lumina Slate (Pro+) series. Its deep, matte-black finish perfectly mimics high-end natural slate, providing a striking yet elegant contrast to the beige stone facade. It elevates the estate's grandeur rather than detracting from it.
High-Yield Invisible Power: By utilizing the expansive multi-hip roof area, we seamlessly integrated a massive 15kW system. The installation was carefully edge-to-edge, ensuring that from the grand front lawn, the roof appears simply as a premium architectural feature, while silently generating an estimated 15,000 kWh annually.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数 (合理预估):

System Type: Grid-Tied Ultra-Luxury Residential

Solar Tiles: Lumina Slate Series (Pro+)

Estimated Installed Capacity: 15 kWp

Estimated Active Roof Area: 250 sq.m

Annual Estimated Output: 15,000 kWh

Annual Environmental Impact: Reduces CO2 emissions by 14.9 tons, equivalent to planting 830 trees annually.
