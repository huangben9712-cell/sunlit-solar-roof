Project Title (网页主标题):

Urban Compact Home Retrofit: Typhoon-Ready Solar Integration

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

Dense Urban Environment & High Winds: Located in a high-density neighborhood in Shanghai, this compact single-family home required a complete roof retrofit. The region experiences annual typhoon seasons with severe wind gusts and torrential rain. The homeowner wanted to achieve energy independence but had very limited roof space. They needed a heavy-duty solution that offered absolute structural security without looking like a bulky industrial add-on in a residential area.
Custom Skylight Integration: The architectural design included a massive custom glass sunroom/skylight cutting directly into the roofline, presenting a complex waterproofing challenge for any solar installation.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Maximum Protection in a Small Footprint: The Storm Guard series was the ultimate fit for this urban retrofit. Its robust, vertical interlocking design (reminiscent of premium standing seam metal roofs) provides superior wind-uplift resistance against typhoons while rapidly shedding heavy rain.
Flawless Custom Detailing: Despite the compact footprint, the system maximizes energy output. Furthermore, our installation logs clearly demonstrate how the Storm Guard tiles were custom-flashed and seamlessly integrated around the large skylight—proving that rugged, typhoon-rated weather protection can easily accommodate premium architectural features.

Technical Highlights & Components - 技术参数 (基于小户型合理预估):

System Type: Urban Residential Retrofit

Solar Tiles: Storm Guard Series (Typhoon-Rated)

Estimated Capacity: 8 kWp (High-Density)

Active Roof Area: 85 sq.m

Key Features: Wind-uplift resistance, extreme water shedding, seamless skylight integration.

Annual Estimated Output: 8,800 kWh
