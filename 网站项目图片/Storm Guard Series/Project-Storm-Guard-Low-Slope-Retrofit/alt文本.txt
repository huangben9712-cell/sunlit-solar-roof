Project Title (网页主标题):

Urban Low-Slope Extension: The Ultimate Flat Roof Solar Solution

The Challenge (Client Needs) - 项目挑战:

The Flat Roof Dilemma: Flat or low-slope roofs (commonly found on urban home extensions, sunrooms, and garages) are notoriously susceptible to water ponding and severe leaks during heavy rain. The homeowner wanted to add solar power, but installing traditional rack-mounted panels would require heavy concrete ballasts, adding dangerous dead weight to the structure and creating traps for debris and stagnant water.

The Sunlit Solution - 解决方案:

Zero-Leak Standing Seam Technology: We deployed the Storm Guard series specifically for its standing-seam interlocking profile. This design is perfect for low-slope applications. Acting as the primary roof, it creates a 100% watertight barrier that actively channels rain away, completely eliminating the risk of water ponding.
Lightweight & Efficient: By eliminating the need for heavy ballasted racking, we significantly reduced the structural load on this urban property. As seen in our wet-surface post-rain photos, the 6kW system flawlessly protects the home extension while silently generating around 6,500 kWh of clean energy per year.
